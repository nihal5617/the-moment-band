About Me

I am Neel Jain, a singer, songwriter, and performer based in Mumbai. Throughout my musical journey, I have had the incredible opportunity to mesmerize audiences with over 1000+ gigs. With a versatile collection of songs, I take pride in singing fluently in 10 languages, allowing me to deliver soulful melodies that transcend cultural boundaries. Music has always been my language of expression, and I am honored to share my passion with you. Join me on this musical adventure as we create unforgettable moments together.
